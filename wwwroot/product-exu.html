1/7 Scale Figure Exusiai: Elite II (Arknights)

Â£159.99

"Leader... No, savior, I pledge to this gun in my hand to protect you until the very end of this world."

From the popular smartphone game "Arknights" comes a scale figure of the Penguin Logistics member Exusiai based on her Elite 2 illustration! Exusiai's windblown hair and outfit, halo and wings representative of the Sankta people and her 8 Laterano guns in the background of the illustration have all been carefully recreated, giving her the appearance of an angel descending onto the battlefield.

The figure is packed with details you won't want to miss, including the meticulous paintwork and texturing of her Laterano guns, the rings of light that appear behind Exusiai in the Elite 2 illustration and the figure base made to look like a pile of debris. Be sure to add her to your collection!
